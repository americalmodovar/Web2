Fantasía

Come on, Mick. You had to know.
Nothing good ever comes of brooms that sprout up legs.



You had to know how they’d reproduce,
how they’d organize, mutinize you never saw them.




In your sleep, flooding stone floors
while your navy blue hat floated away?




They marched in time in my sleep, Mickey.
Night after night after night.


—LW Lindquist
